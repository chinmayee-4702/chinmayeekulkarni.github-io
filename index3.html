Some Of Our Initiatives

"Health and Hygiene during Menstruation" awareness campaign

In hopes of helping the underprivileged parts of society, Paakhi- a social initiative founded by MindSpark, conducted a 'Health and Hygiene during Menstruation' awareness campaign in the PMC School, Hadapsar, on 12th March. The school girls were informed about personal hygiene, problems faced due to lack of nutrition during periods and were given sanitary pads to encourage good hygiene practices. MindSpark urges everyone to break the silence, and help build awareness about menstrual health and hygiene.

Breast Cancer Workshop

Last Year under Paakhi, in collaboration with Robin Hood Army and Prashanti Cancer care, Mindspark conducted breast cancer detection workshop in different slum areas of Pune. The people detected with this disease were provided with free treatment.

Techno Literacy

If women are expected to do the same work as men, we must teach them the same things. With this purpose in mind and to commemorate Women's day, MS enthusiastically collaborated with Deep Griha Society, an independent charitable organization in teaching women in slum areas the correct usage of mobile phones and technology in today's world . Step by step, MindSpark aims to strengthen the foundation of technical literacy for a better future.
